who we are

We help our clients develop comprehensive brand strategies that allow for greater exposure and connection with their target customer bases. We focus on both the brand’s visual identity and voice, ensuring that people see and hear a true reflection of the brand’s heart and soul.

Our services

We offer clients with customized solutions to fit their individual branding needs. We specialize in the whole spectrum of branding, including:

  • Logo design and identity development
  • Website design and construction
  • Brand collateral
  • Marketing strategies and campaigns
  • Content creation
  • Packaging design

our community

At WeSuperseed, we have begun building a network of talented, multi-cultural and innovative individuals who are interested in brand strategy and design. Our aim is to create a collaborative platform for creative thinkers that are looking for work or that require assistance with a project on hand. The aim of this platform is to bridge and build a tighter knit creative community, while the growth will increase the manpower available allowing us to offer our clients a wider range of services and shorter response times.

We are always looking for creative minds that align with our vision and join our Community. If you think you would be a good fit and have the skills and determination to help drive our platform forward, or if you need assistance on a current project and are looking for help from likeminded creative individuals, let us know how we can work together.
